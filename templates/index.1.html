Melinda Oakeson

12/30/2023
Melinda Oakeson, a 46-year-old woman who was reported missing on Oct. 16, was found dead by a group of hikers on a mountainside near Craggy Wash.

Oakeson had been living out of her vehicle for an undetermined amount of time, and the sheriff's office did not immediately provide details on how she died or how she got to the location.
